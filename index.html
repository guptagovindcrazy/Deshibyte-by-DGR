Taste of Tradition


Bringing the authentic and forgotten flavors of traditional Bihar straight to your doorstep. We use pure ingredients, traditional recipes, and manufacture with deep love and hygiene. Every bite takes you on a nostalgic journey back to your roots.

A Note from the Founder


"I started Deshibyte because I realized our rich, native Bihari snacks were getting lost in a world of highly processed junk food. I wanted people everywhere to experience the exact same pure Sattu and crunchy Thekua I grew up eating at my grandmother's house. Deshibyte isn't just a business; it's an effort to preserve our heritage on a plate."
